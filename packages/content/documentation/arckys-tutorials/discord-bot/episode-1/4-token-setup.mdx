## Token Setup

- In the side menu click on "Bot" then under "Token" click "Reset Token".
<ImageWithCaption src="/images/documentation/arcky-tutorials/discord-bot-tutorial/Client Token.png" caption="Client Secret" align="left" size="large"/> 
<br/>
Pase this token without quotes after `TOKEN` in your `.env` file.
<br/>You may also replace `APPLICATION_ID` with the `Client ID`
<br/>Do the same for `SERVER_ID` with the ID of the server you invited the bot to.
```plaintext:.env
TOKEN=PASTE_TOKEN
CLIENT_ID=APPLICATION_ID
GUILD_ID=SERVER_ID
```
<Callout type="warning">**<Rd>DO NOT SHARE YOUR TOKEN WITH ANYONE OR THEY WILL BE ABLE TO MANIPULATE AND CONTROL YOUR BOT INSTEAD OF ONLY YOU. You can Reset your tOKEN any time in worst case scenario!</Rd>**</Callout>
