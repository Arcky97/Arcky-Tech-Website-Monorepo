## Other Parameters

<InputTextArea label="Other Fields" placeholder="No Extra Fields" width={50}/>
Any custom parameters present in your **Berry Plant PBS files** that are <Gr>not recognized by the editor</Gr> will automatically be grouped under the <Or>Other Fields</Or> section.
<br/>
These values are preserved <Yl>exactly as they appear</Yl> in the original file, without modification or reformatting.
<br/>
When saving, the editor will write these fields back unchanged ensuring that <Gr>plugin-specific</Gr> or <Gr>engine-modified</Gr> data is never lost or corrupted.
