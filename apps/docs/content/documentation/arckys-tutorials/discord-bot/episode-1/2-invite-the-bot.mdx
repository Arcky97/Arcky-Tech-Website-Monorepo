## Invite the Bot 

- In the side menu click on "OAuth2" and scroll down to "OAuth2 URL Generator" section.
<ImageWithCaption src="/images/documentation/arcky-tutorials/discord-bot-tutorial/OAuth2 URL Generator.png" caption="OAuth2 URL Generator" align="left" size="full"/> 
- In *Scopes* enable 
  - "bot"
  - "application.commands"
<ImageWithCaption src="/images/documentation/arcky-tutorials/discord-bot-tutorial/Scopes.png" caption="Scopes" align="left" size="small"/> 
- In *Bot Permissions* enable
  - Send Messages
  - Use Slash commands
  - Read Message History
<ImageWithCaption src="/images/documentation/arcky-tutorials/discord-bot-tutorial/Bot Permissions.png" caption="Bot Permissions" align="left" size="medium"/> 
<br/>
- Copy the Generated URL and paste it in your browser to invite your bot to your server.
<ImageWithCaption src="/images/documentation/arcky-tutorials/discord-bot-tutorial/Generated URL.png" caption="Generated URL" align="left" size="large"/> 
- The bot has now joined the server and appears offline in the member list
<ImageWithCaption src="/images/documentation/arcky-tutorials/discord-bot-tutorial/Bot Join Offline.png" caption="Bot Join Offline" align="left" size="small"/>
